Homemade Ravioli Recipe

Make dinner easy with a cheesy ravioli bake recipe that combines ground beef and frozen ravioli. Prep four simple ingredients for this frozen ravioli
bake in ten minutes. Save even more time by making your ravioli pasta bake ahead of time and freezing it until your family is ready to dig in.

Full Disclaimer: Credit to Pillsbury for the recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lb of ground beef
  • 2 jars (25.5 oz each) Muir Glen organic marinara pasta sauce
  • 2 bags (18 oz each) frozen cheese ravioli
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil leaves

Steps:

  1. Heat your oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit. Spray a 13x9-inch (3 quarts) baking disk with cooking spray.
  2. In a 10-inch skillet, cook the ground beef over medium-high heat, stirring frequently, until brown. Drain. Then stir in 2 jars of the marinara sauce.
  3. Spread 1 cup sauce mixture in baking dish. Have ready 2 bags (18 oz each) frozen cheese ravioli and 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese (8 oz).
    Place 1 layer frozen ravioli evenly over sauce in baking dish. Top with one-third of the remaining sauce and one-third of the cheese. Repeat for a total of 3 layers.
  4. Cover with foil. Bake 40 minutes. Remove foil; bake about 5 minutes until cheese is melted. Serve garnished with 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil leaves.

Nutrition Facts:

510 Calories, 23g Total Fat, 23g Protein, 53g Total Carbohydrate, 6g Sugars
